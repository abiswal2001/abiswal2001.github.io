Asim Biswal

I am a second year Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Joseph Gonzalez. My current research interest is studying the intersection of large language models and databases. Specifically, I am interested in leveraging the full potential of structured and unstructured data and their patterns of interactions using language models. This includes working on problems such as Text2SQL as well as the new paradigm TAG (Table Augmented Generation).

I previously graduated from the University of California, Berkeley with a B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. During this time, I worked in the Sky Computing Lab on problems such as fair resource allocation and wide-area networking.
